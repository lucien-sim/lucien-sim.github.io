Below is a map of tax lots in Manhattan in 2019, which have an elevator and more than 100 residential units. The points are colored by the number of residential units, with yellow colors representing a larger number of units. The map is interactive: when you hover over a point with the mouse, you'll see the tax lot's BBL, address, and number of units. If you zoom in far enough, you'll also also see each building's footprint.
